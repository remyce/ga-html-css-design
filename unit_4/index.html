About

Remyce is a designer based in London specialising in Branding, Graphic Design and Web Development. She first studied Interactive Art and Design in North London, developing skills in Web Design, Media, Photography and Still Art. Remyce has always been passionate in visual design, after spending a few years in fashion retail as a brand specialist and visual merchandiser, she then graduated from the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly.

Currently working as a freelance Web Developer, Remyce loves to create beautiful digital experiences for a range of businesses.

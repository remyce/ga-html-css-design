Inc.

Founded by Lisa Toole in 2004 as a solution to her own skin allergies, our certified organic products have become the premium choice for those who've tried everything in their search for skin confidence.

With one of the cleanest and most transparent ingredient policies on the market, Inc. help their global following of sensitive souls navigate the tricky world of skincare.

All Pai products are formulated and manufactured in-house in London by our team of organic cosmetic specialists, and are Soil Association, Vegan Society and Leaping Bunny certified.
